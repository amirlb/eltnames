Reversed Semantle

Eltnames is a word-guessing game created by Yaron and Amir.

It is similar to Semantle but in reverse: you pick a word, and the computer tries to guess it!

Whenever the computer guesses a word, you score it for how close it was from 0 to 9, until it finds the right word.

You indicate a correct guess with by giving the ✔️ mark.
